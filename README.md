如果掉腦袋不只是個意外，而是一種超能力呢？

《Mask Man》是一款以物理機制為核心的硬核平台跳躍遊戲。你將扮演一名擁有奇特能力的不死族，你的骷髏頭不僅僅是你的容貌，更是你通往終點的唯一密碼。

在這個充滿岩漿、深水與懸浮島嶼的危險世界中，你必須學會「身首異處」的藝術。將你的頭部投擲到遠方，並決定是要將它召回，還是讓你的身體化作殘影，飛向那顆指引方向的頭顱。

核心玩法：

* 身首分離： 隨時投擲你的骷髏頭，探索肉身無法觸及的路徑。

* 靈魂換位： 瞬間移動到頭部的位置，利用位移跨越岩漿與致命陷阱。

* 極限操作： 掌握二段跳與攀爬技巧，在水域與高塔間穿梭。

* 計時挑戰： 運用你的智慧與反應，以最短的時間抵達紅旗終點！

操作說明：

* A / D： 左右移動

* Space： 跳躍 / 二段跳 (掌握時機) / 游泳

*  F /  V ：上下樓梯 

* 滑鼠左鍵： 投擲骷髏頭 / 召回

* 滑鼠右鍵： 身體瞬移至骷髏頭位置 (需在無頭狀態)

Game Title: Mask 

Tagline: Losing your head has never been this useful.

About the Game:

What if losing your head wasn't a catastrophe, but a superpower?

Mask Man is a physics-based, precision platformer where your skull is more than just a face—it’s the anchor of your soul and the key to your survival.

In a world filled with lethal lava, deep waters, and floating ruins, you must master the art of detachment. Throw your head across chasms, then decide: do you call it back to your shoulders, or do you let your body vanish into a ghost-dash, reappearing wherever your head has landed?

Key Features:

* Decapitation Mechanics: Throw your skull to scout and reach areas your body can't access.

* Translocation: Teleport your body to your head's position to bypass deadly traps and lava.

* Precision Platforming: Master double jumps, ladder climbing, and swimming through challenging environments.

* Speedrun Challenge: Every second counts. Find the most efficient path to the red flag!

Controls:

* A / D: Move Left/Right

* Space: Jump / Double Jump (Timed)  / Swim

* F / V: Ladder Up/Down

* Left Click: Throw / Recall Skull

* Right Click: Teleport Body to Skull (When Headless)
